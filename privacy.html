Privacy Policy

Slings and Arrows · Effective June 28, 2026

Slings and Arrows does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no ads, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Information we collect

None. The app does not gather any information that identifies you, and it does not transmit any data off your device. We have no servers that receive your information.

Data stored on your device

Your high scores and game settings (such as aim sensitivity and handedness) are saved locally on your device using the system’s standard app storage, so the game remembers your preferences between sessions. This information never leaves your device and is removed if you delete the app.

Tracking & advertising

We do not track you across apps or websites, and we show no advertisements. The app contains no third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

Children’s privacy

Because the app collects no data at all, it is safe for players of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes (for example, if a future version adds a new feature), we will update this page and revise the effective date above.
